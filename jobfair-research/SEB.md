## Relevance for Tom (Security/CTO Profile)

**MEDIUM-HIGH FIT** — Banking security is demanding, but SEB is less security-focused than Swedbank:

1. **Kubernetes + container security** — directly relevant to DevOps/infrastructure expertise
2. **AI/ML for risk** — AI Engineer role in Finance Risk & Data Tribe
3. **Banking security** — API security, fraud detection, AML
4. **GitOps + IaC** — modern infrastructure practices
5. **200+ internal applications** — broad attack surface
6. **Tribes model** — agile, cross-functional teams

**Note:** SEB's security function seems less mature/visible than Swedbank's. The AI Engineer role might be more interesting than pure security.
