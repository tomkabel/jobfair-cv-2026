## Systems Managed (100+ ICT Services)

SMIT manages the IT behind Estonia's internal security:

- **National Population Register** — identity management, birth registration
- **Police & Border Guard IT** — law enforcement, criminal investigations, border surveillance, Schengen Information System
- **Document services** — passports, ID cards, residence cards, visas, e-Residency biometrics
- **112 Emergency systems** — Emergency Response Centre infrastructure
- **Rescue Board IT** — crisis management, crisis hotline
- **E-voting infrastructure**
- **EU systems** — Schengen Information System, EU Entry/Exit System (EES)

### Client agencies:
1. Police and Border Guard Board (PPA)
2. Estonian Rescue Board
3. Estonian Emergency Response Centre

5. Ministry of the Interior

## Security Posture (CRITICAL)

SMIT operates at the heart of Estonian state security:

- **SSH PrivX Just-in-Time PAM** deployed (Oct 2025) — zero-trust access, session recording, NIS2/GDPR compliance
- **Guardtime partnership** — KSI blockchain for independent audit of distributed systems, tamper-proof audit trails
- **PKI / eID infrastructure** — certificate management for eMRTD issuance and border control
- **Biometric systems** — mobile biometric collection for e-residents (X Infotech, 2026)
- **Dedicated Security Unit**
- **Background checks** required for hiring
- **NIS2 and GDPR compliant**

**Data handled:** forensic evidence, passports, biometric data, criminal records, investigation files, government personnel records
