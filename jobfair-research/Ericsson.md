## Security Aspects (CRITICAL)

Ericsson is positioning itself as a leader in telecom cybersecurity:

- **Ericsson Security Manager (ESM)** — cyber defense platform with telecom-specific threat intelligence
- **Operational Technology (OT) Security Lead** — active role in Tallinn
- **CISA alignment** — operationalizing US CISA guidance for hardening 5G against APTs
- **Zero Trust Architecture** — launched zero-trust solution for private 5G networks
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography** — AT&T-Ericsson joint paper (Mar 2026) on PQC in 5G RAN
- **6G Security** — Ericsson presenting at ETSI Security Conference 2025
- **Nation-state threat defense** — defending against APTs targeting telecom infrastructure

### Estonia-Specific Context
Estonia is on NATO's eastern flank with Russia. Telecom infrastructure = national security. Ericsson's Tallinn unit operates at the intersection of manufacturing security, 5G network security, and geopolitical reality.
