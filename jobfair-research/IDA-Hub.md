## Studio Complex (Under Construction)

**Jõhvi Film Campus** — largest studio complex in the Nordics/Baltics, targeted completion **August 2026**:

- Studio 1: 2,000 m² sound stage, 20m ceiling
- Studio 2: Multifunctional hall (1,100 seated / 2,500 standing)
- Post-production: editing rooms, sound studio, VFX, color grading
- 100+ rental workstations, co-working center
- Solar panels, EV charging, LEED sustainability
