## Estonia's National Cybersecurity Context

Estonia is the world's most advanced e-governance nation:
- **100% of government services are digital** (as of Jan 2025)
- **X-Road** — decentralized data exchange connecting all government systems
- **e-ID** covers 99% of the population
- **2007 cyberattacks** — first state-level cyber warfare, catalyzed security-by-design approach
- **NATO CCDCOE** located in Tallinn
- **Cybersecurity Strategy 2024–2030: "Cyber-conscious Estonia"**
- **Post-quantum cryptography** roadmap under development (Cybernetica, Jan 2026)
