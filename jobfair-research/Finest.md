## Notable Projects (CRITICAL STATE SYSTEMS)

Finest builds and maintains nationally critical Estonian e-government systems:

- **DigiDoc** — digital identity and document signing mobile app (e-ID system)
- **Mobile-ID** solution
- **eesti.ee** — Estonian state portal
- **ARIS2** — National Road Administration traffic registry (110M+ entries)
- **National Unemployment Fund** — benefits processing (~€50M managed)
- **Riigikogu (Parliament)** — verbatim records system
- **Police & Border Guard** — information systems (visa, immigration, traffic violations)
- **GIS-112** — National Emergency Call Centre solution
- **Maksekeskus** — in-house payment platform (integral to Estonian e-commerce)
