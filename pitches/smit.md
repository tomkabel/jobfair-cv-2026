# SMIT — Elevator Pitch (Liftikõne)

**Target:** SMIT (Ministry of the Interior IT Centre) engineering teams
**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

---

## The Pitch

**[HOOK — 5s]**
"Police systems, border control, emergency services — SMIT runs the digital backbone of Estonian state security. A vulnerability here isn't a bug report, it's a national security incident."

**[WHO I AM — 10s]**
"I'm Tom Kristian Abel — security researcher and CTO. I founded ProksiAbel OÜ doing offensive security consulting, and I'm CTO at MatX.ee. My team won FELLIN HÄKK 2026 by finding the root causes of vulnerabilities, not just patching symptoms."

**[WHY SMIT — 15s]**
"Public sector IT is where security has the highest stakes. You're not protecting revenue — you're protecting people. The systems that run Estonia's police, border, and rescue operations need engineers who think about security as a first-class concern, not a compliance exercise. I've worked on offensive security for years — building the tools and techniques that adversaries use — so I know exactly what needs to be defended against."

**[WHAT I BRING — 15s]**
"I bring a security-first engineering mindset backed by production experience. I've reverse-engineered complex architectures, bypassed detection systems, and built concurrent tooling for vulnerability discovery. My TalTech project is a production-grade async API with Docker, PostgreSQL, and JWT auth. I'm a native Estonian speaker, and I understand that public sector IT requires not just technical skill but the trust that comes with working on systems that protect citizens."

**[CLOSING — 5s]**
"SMIT protects Estonia from the inside. I want to make sure the digital tools our security forces rely on are as resilient as the people who use them."

---

## Delivery Notes

- **Deliver in Estonian if possible** — SMIT serves the Ministry of the Interior, Estonian is the working language
- **"A national security incident"** — immediately raises the stakes, shows you understand the gravity
- **Pause** after "you're protecting people" — this is the emotional core
- **Emphasize** "root causes, not just patching symptoms" — shows architectural thinking
- **Final sentence** — "as resilient as the people who use them" is a strong closer

## Key Topics to Have Ready

- Critical national infrastructure security
- Government systems modernization and legacy security
- Estonia's cybersecurity model and how SMIT fits into it
- Security clearance and trust requirements for public sector
- How internship pathways lead to meaningful public sector careers
- Integration with X-tee and national data exchange layer
