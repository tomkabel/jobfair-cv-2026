## Delivery Notes

- **Lead with confidence** — banking teams respect directness
- **Pause** after "not just compliance checkboxes" — this signals you understand their pain
- **Emphasize** "millions of people trust with their money" — connects your work to real impact
- **On PSD2** — this is a specific, technical talking point that shows you've done homework
- If asked about languages: "I work primarily in Go and JavaScript, but I'm comfortable across the stack"

- Azure migration security architecture
- SIEM optimization (Exabeam platform, SOAR, XDR next steps)
- PSD2 API security and open banking attack surfaces
- Anti-Financial Crime + Cybersecurity team structure in Estonia
- DevSecOps in Jenkins/Azure DevOps pipelines
