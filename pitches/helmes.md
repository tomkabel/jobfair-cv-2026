# Helmes — Elevator Pitch (Liftikõne)

**Target:** Helmes cybersecurity & engineering teams
**Duration:** 45–60 seconds

---

## The Pitch

**[HOOK — 5s]**
"OECD security audits, PKI systems for Keyfactor, ISO 27001 across the board — Helmes doesn't just build software, they build trust. That's exactly where I want to be."

**[WHO I AM — 10s]**
"I'm Tom Kristian Abel — security researcher, founder of ProksiAbel OÜ, and CTO at MatX.ee. I've spent years doing offensive security consulting: penetration testing, vulnerability research, reverse engineering. My team won FELLIN HÄKK 2026 by thinking like attackers and building like engineers."

**[WHY HELMES — 15s]**
"Helmes is one of the few companies in Estonia that has security embedded in every project team — not bolted on as an afterthought. Your Security Leads model is how security *should* work. The work you did on the OECD audit — pen testing, architecture analysis, cryptographic audits — is exactly the kind of high-impact security consulting I've been doing through ProksiAbel. I understand the consulting model, the client relationships, and the pressure of delivering security in regulated environments."

**[WHAT I BRING — 15s]**
"I bring an offensive security mindset backed by real production experience. I've built concurrent tooling in Golang for automated reconnaissance, bypassed client-side detection systems, and reverse-engineered complex JavaScript architectures. My TalTech microdegree project demonstrates full-stack engineering — async APIs, Docker, PostgreSQL, JWT auth. I'm also a native Estonian speaker, which matters for your government and NIS2 compliance projects."

**[CLOSING — 5s]**
"Helmes has 30 years of building software that matters. I want to make sure the next 30 years are built on a security foundation that can withstand anything."

## Delivery Notes

- **Lead with their achievements** — shows you've done your homework and respect their work
- **Emphasize** "not bolted on as an afterthought" — validates their approach
- **Connect ProksiAbel to Helmes model** — natural bridge showing you understand their business
- **On NIS2** — shows awareness of current EU regulatory landscape
- **Final sentence** — ties your long-term value to their 30-year legacy

## Key Topics to Have Ready

- Security-by-design embedded in project teams (Security Lead model)
- OECD audit case study — what a full-scope security engagement looks like
- Keyfactor PKI/EJBCA — Common Criteria, FIPS, eIDAS compliance
- NIS2 directive readiness and what it means for Helmes' clients
- Autonomous team model — how security fits into teams that function like small companies
- ISO 27001 certified processes and continuous improvement
