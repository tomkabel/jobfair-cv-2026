- **SCADA/OT mention** — shows you understand the unique challenges of industrial environments
- **Pause** after "most IT security professionals don't understand OT security" — this is your niche
- **On energy independence** — connects to Estonia's national strategy, shows broader awareness
- **Final sentence** — "resilient as the physical infrastructure" is a strong engineering-minded closer
