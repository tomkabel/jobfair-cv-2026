"200 million customers, 50 countries, three separate security teams — and you're about to add autonomous vehicles to that attack surface. That's the kind of problem I live for."

"I'm Tom Kristian Abel — security researcher turned CTO. I founded ProksiAbel OÜ doing offensive security consulting, and I'm currently CTO at MatX.ee. "Bolt is unique because your security challenge scales with every new product line. Ride-hailing was the beginning. "I bring the CTO perspective — I've led technology teams, made architecture decisions, and shipped under pressure. I don't just break things; I understand *why* they break and how to fix them at the root. My TalTech microdegree project is a production-grade async API with Docker, Redis queues, and JWT auth — the kind of infrastructure Bolt runs at scale."

"Bolt is building the future of European mobility. I want to make sure that future is secure — from the first e-scooter unlock to the last autonomous ride."

## Delivery Notes

- **Pause** after "200 million customers" — let the scale land
- **Emphasize** "I know how to find what others miss" — this is the differentiator
- **Slow down** on the final sentence — it's the one they remember 2 hours later
- **Eye contact** with the most senior engineer in the group
- If asked about the hackathon: "We found the root cause, not just the symptom — that's always my approach"

- IoT fleet security (MQTT on e-scooters)
- Autonomous vehicle security (NVIDIA/Pony.ai partnerships)
